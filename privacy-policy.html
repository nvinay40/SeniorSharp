Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

  • Store your game scores locally on your phone

DATA YOU CONTROL:

When you share scores with family, you edit the message yourself. We don't store or access that shared information.

OFFLINE FIRST:

The app works completely offline. No internet required. No data is ever sent to our servers.
