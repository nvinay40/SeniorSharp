# Brain Games for Seniors

A free, offline brain training app designed specifically for seniors aged 65+. 
Built with accessibility and privacy as first-class concerns.

## Problem

Most brain training apps are designed for younger users. They have small fonts, 
complicated interfaces, aggressive notifications, and collect extensive user data. 
There are many brain game apps that dominate the market but ignore the cognitive needs 
of older adults. Seniors deserve an app built for them—not a scaled-down version 
of something else.

## Solution

Three simple games. Five to ten minutes per day. That's it.

- **Card Matching**: Tap cards to find matching pairs. Trains memory and focus.
- **Sequence Matching**: Watch and repeat patterns. Improves attention and recall.
- **Simple Arithmetic**: Quick mental math problems. Keeps numerical thinking sharp.

All games are paced by the user. No timers. No pressure. No artificial urgency.

## Key Features

✓ **Built for Seniors**
- Large, easy-to-read fonts (16px minimum)
- High contrast colors reduce eye strain
- Simple tap controls—no complex gestures
- No time pressure or performance anxiety

✓ **Privacy First**
- Zero data collection—we don't track you
- Works completely offline—no internet required
- Local storage only—scores never leave your phone
- No ads, no analytics, no third-party tracking

✓ **Multilingual**
- English and Hindi at launch
- Regional languages planned for v1.1

✓ **Family Connection**
- Share scores with custom messages via WhatsApp/text
- Family can monitor progress without privacy loss
- You control what you share

✓ **Completely Free**
- No ads
- No hidden costs
- No in-app purchases
