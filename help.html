1. Getting Started

First Launch

When you open Senior Sharp for the first time, you will be asked to:

  1. Choose your language — English, हिन्दी (Hindi), or ꥼુ꧁રાતી (Gujarati)
  2. Enter your name (optional) — used for a personal greeting on the Home screen
Tip: You can change your name and language at any time from the Settings tab at the bottom of the screen.

The Home Screen

After setup, you will see the Home screen with:

  • A greeting based on the time of day
  • A Today’s Challenge card — tap it to start your workout
  • A This Week section showing which days you played
  • A Good to Know tip about brain health

2. Your Daily Workout

Each workout takes about 5–10 minutes and consists of 3 games played one after another.

Choosing Difficulty

Before starting, choose how challenging you want today’s workout to be:

  • Easy — smaller grids, simpler questions, shorter sequences
  • Medium — a moderate challenge suitable for most players
  • Hard — larger grids, harder maths, longer sequences
Tip: Start with Easy and move up when you feel confident. There is no wrong choice!

Game Order

The three games always play in this order:

  1. Card Matching
  2. Sequence Tapping
  3. Quick Maths

Between Rounds

Each game has 2 rounds. A short 3-second countdown appears between rounds so you can take a breath and get ready.

Sounds and Haptics

Senior Sharp plays sounds and gentle vibrations (haptic feedback) as you tap buttons and complete actions. Make sure your phone’s media volume is turned up to hear the sounds.

Workout Complete

After all three games, you will see your total score, a breakdown by game, and options to share your result or return home.

3. The Three Games

Game 1 — Card Matching

A classic memory game. Cards are placed face-down on a grid. Tap two cards to flip them — if they match (same emoji), they stay open. Find all matching pairs to complete the round.

Rounds: 2 rounds with a 3-second break between them.

Grid size by difficulty:

Easy 2×2 grid (2 pairs)

Medium 4×4 grid (8 pairs)

Hard 6×6 grid (18 pairs)

Tip: Remember where you saw each card before flipping a second one. Go slowly — accuracy matters more than speed.

Game 2 — Sequence Tapping

A Simon Says-style memory game. Watch tiles light up in a sequence, then tap them in the same order. The sequence grows longer in the second round.

Rounds: 2 rounds.

Starting sequence length:

Easy 4 tiles

Medium 6 tiles

Hard 9 tiles

If you tap the wrong tile, it flashes red — you can keep going, but each mistake reduces your score. Use the “Watch Again” button to replay the sequence before you tap.

Tip: Say each emoji out loud as you watch the sequence — it helps your brain remember the order.

Game 3 — Quick Maths

Simple arithmetic questions with multiple-choice answers. Tap the correct answer for each question.

Rounds: 2 rounds.

Question types by difficulty:

Easy Single-digit addition & subtraction

Medium Two-digit addition & subtraction

Hard Larger numbers including multiplication

Tip: Take a moment to read the question carefully before tapping an answer. You cannot change your answer once tapped.

4. Scoring

Points are awarded at the end of each game based on performance and difficulty. Higher difficulty means more points possible.

Card Matching

You earn points for each pair found. If you find all pairs with 2 or fewer wrong flips, you receive a 25% accuracy bonus.

5. Tracking Your Progress

Tap the Progress tab (bar chart icon) at the bottom of the screen to see how you are doing.

What You’ll See

  • Workouts Completed — total number of full workouts you have finished
  • Total Score — all points earned across every workout
  • Streak — how many days in a row you have played
  • This Week — dots showing which days (Monday to Sunday) you played this week

Sharing Your Progress

Tap the Share Progress button to send your stats to family or friends via WhatsApp, SMS, or any other app installed on your phone.

Tip: Playing every day builds your streak. Try to keep the dots going all week — it is a great motivator!

6. Settings

Tap the Settings tab (gear icon) at the bottom of the screen.

Your Name and Language

Both are shown right at the top of the Settings screen:

  • Your Name — tap the text box and type to change it
  • Language — tap EN, HI, or GU to switch instantly between English, Hindi, and Gujarati

Display

  • Theme — choose Light, Dark, or System (follows your phone’s display setting)
  • Text Size — choose Small, Medium, or Large to make text easier to read

Reminders

Set a daily time to receive a notification reminding you to do your brain workout.

About

  • Links to the Privacy Policy and this Help Guide
  • The app version number is shown at the very bottom of the Settings screen

7. Reminders

Senior Sharp can send you a daily reminder so you never miss your brain workout.

Setting Up a Reminder

  1. Go to Settings → Reminders → Daily Reminder
  2. Turn on the reminder and choose your preferred time
  3. Tap Save

You may also receive special notifications for:

  • Streak milestones — when you hit 3, 7, 14, or 30 days in a row
  • Inactivity nudge — a friendly reminder if you have not played in a few days
Tip: If you are not receiving notifications, open your phone’s Settings → Apps → Senior Sharp → Notifications and make sure notifications are allowed.

8. Frequently Asked Questions

Is Senior Sharp free? Yes, Senior Sharp is completely free to download and play. There are no in-app purchases or subscriptions.
Do I need an internet connection to play? No. All three games work fully offline. You only need an internet connection to download or update the app from the Play Store.
Do I need to create an account? No account is needed. Your progress is saved automatically on your device.
What languages does the app support? Senior Sharp supports English, Hindi, and Gujarati. You can switch languages at any time in Settings.
What happens if I close the app during a workout? If you leave mid-workout, your progress for that session will not be saved. Your overall history and streak are not affected.
Can I play more than once a day? Yes! You can play as many times as you like each day.
How do I change the language? Go to Settings and tap EN, HI, or GU next to the Language option. The app changes immediately.
Will my progress be lost if I uninstall the app? Yes — all progress is stored on your device. Uninstalling the app will permanently delete your history and streak.
The text is too small. Can I make it bigger? Yes. Go to Settings → Display → Text Size and select Large.
I am not hearing any sounds. What should I do? Make sure your phone’s media volume is turned up — not just the ring volume. You can adjust media volume by pressing the volume buttons while a sound is playing.
How is my streak counted? You must complete at least one full workout each calendar day to extend your streak. Missing a day resets the streak to zero.
How do I contact support? Email us at nihalani.vinay@gmail.com and we will get back to you as soon as possible.
