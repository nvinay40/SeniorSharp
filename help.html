What is Senior Sharp?

Senior Sharp is a free daily games app specially designed for senior citizens. It helps you keep your mind active and engaged with fun, easy-to-play memory and thinking games.

Every day, you play a short workout of 3 games that takes just 5–10 minutes. Playing daily is a great mental habit — just like exercise keeps the body active, daily games keep the mind busy and engaged.

💡 Good to Know: A daily mental challenge is a great habit for senior citizens. Regular play keeps the mind busy, engaged, and entertained.

How to Start

  1. Open the Senior Sharp app on your phone.
  2. Choose your preferred language — English or हिन्दी.
  3. Enter your name (optional) and tap Continue.
  4. On the home screen, tap START WORKOUT to begin.
  5. Choose your difficulty level: Easy, Medium, or Hard.
  6. Play all 3 games and see your total score!

The 3 Games

Game 1
🃏 Card Matching

A classic memory game. Cards are placed face-down on the screen. Flip two cards at a time to find matching pairs. Remember where each card is to match faster!

How to Play

  1. Tap any card to flip it over.
  2. Tap a second card to try and find its match.
  3. If they match, both cards stay face-up. ✅
  4. If they don't match, both cards flip back — remember where they were!
  5. Find all matching pairs to complete the round.
  6. Complete 2 rounds to finish the game.

Difficulty Levels

LevelGrid SizePairs to Find
Easy2×22 pairs
Medium4×48 pairs
Hard6×618 pairs
💡 Tip: Take your time to look at all the cards before tapping. The more you remember, the higher your score!
Game 2
👆 Sequence Tapping

A memory sequence game. Coloured tiles light up one by one in a specific order. Watch carefully, then tap the tiles back in the same order.

How to Play

  1. Watch the tiles light up in sequence — don't tap yet!
  2. When it says "Your Turn!", tap the tiles in the same order.
  3. Get it right to complete the round. Get it wrong and the round ends.
  4. If you forget the sequence, tap WATCH AGAIN to replay it.
  5. Complete 2 rounds to finish the game.

Difficulty Levels

LevelStarting Sequence Length
Easy3 tiles
Medium4 tiles
Hard5 tiles
💡 Tip: Say the colours out loud as they light up — it helps your brain remember the order!
Game 3
🔢 Quick Maths

A mental arithmetic game. Simple addition and subtraction questions appear on screen. Choose the correct answer from 4 options as quickly as you can!

How to Play

  1. Read the maths question shown on screen (e.g. 7 + 5 = ?).
  2. Tap the correct answer from the 4 options shown.
  3. Answer before the timer runs out!
  4. Each round has 4 questions.
  5. Complete 2 rounds to finish the game.

Difficulty Levels

LevelNumbers UsedTime per Question
EasySingle digit (1–9)15 seconds
MediumTwo digit (10–99)20 seconds
HardThree digit (100–999)25 seconds
💡 Tip: Don't rush — it's better to take 2 extra seconds and get it right than to tap the wrong answer quickly!

How Scoring Works

Each game gives you points. Your total score is the sum of all 3 games.

Card Matching Score

  • 10 points per matching pair found
  • +25% bonus if you make 2 or fewer mistakes
  • Higher difficulty = more points (Medium ×1.5, Hard ×2)

Sequence Tapping Score

  • Points for each round completed correctly
  • Higher difficulty = more points

Quick Maths Score

  • 10 points per correct answer
  • +20% bonus if you get all 4 questions right in a round
  • Higher difficulty = more points (Medium ×1.5, Hard ×2)
💡 Tip: Play on Easy first to learn the games. Once you're comfortable, switch to Medium or Hard to earn more points!

Daily Reminders

Senior Sharp can send you a daily reminder so you never forget your brain workout.

  1. Tap the ☰ Menu button on the home screen.
  2. Tap Settings.
  3. Tap Notifications.
  4. Turn notifications ON and choose your preferred reminder time.

You will receive a daily notification at your chosen time reminding you to play.

Tracking Your Progress

See how your scores improve over time!

  1. Tap the ☰ Menu button on the home screen.
  2. Tap Progress.

You will see:

  • Your total games played and best score
  • Your current streak — how many days in a row you've played
  • A weekly bar chart showing your daily scores

Tap SHARE PROGRESS to share your progress image with family and friends on WhatsApp or any other app.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Do I need the internet to play?

No. Senior Sharp works completely offline. No internet connection is needed to play the games.

Q: Is the app free?

Yes, Senior Sharp is completely free. There are no hidden charges, subscriptions, or advertisements.

Q: Do I need to create an account?

No account is needed. Just open the app and start playing. All your progress is saved on your phone automatically.

Q: What happens if I don't finish the workout?

If you leave the app mid-workout, the session is marked as incomplete. Your progress for that day won't be counted. Simply start again from the home screen.

Q: Can I play more than once a day?

Yes! You can play as many times as you like. However, only one workout per day counts towards your streak.

Q: How do I change the language to Hindi?

Go to Menu → Settings → Language and tap हिन्दी. The whole app will switch to Hindi immediately.

Q: The text is too small. Can I make it bigger?

Yes! Go to Menu → Settings → Text Size and tap Large.

Q: Will my scores be deleted if I uninstall the app?

Yes. All data is stored on your phone. If you uninstall the app, your scores and progress will be lost. Reinstalling starts fresh.
